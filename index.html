    Student Profile

    M.Sc Computer Science

    The M.Sc. Computer Science programme, is a four-semester course which aims at imparting quality educationin core Computer Science, so that the students are prepared to face the challenges ofthe highly competitive IT industryas well as carry out research and development. The objective of the programme is to imbibe sound knowledge of theory and hands on practical skills various areas of Computer Science. The program aims imparting advanced courses in Computer Science.

    Master of Computer Application

    Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a fulltime six semester course (3 year program) / four - semester course (2 year program started from the year 2020), which includes one semester of professional training in the industry. The objective is to impart quality education in Computer Science and its applications, so that students are well prepared to face the challenges of the highly competitive IT industry. The course structure ensures overall development of the student, while concentrating on imparting technical skills required for an IT profession.
